CONVERSATIONS ACROSS THE LIFESPAN

HAVE YOU HAD "THE TALK?"

As Americans are living longer, initiating discussions around aging and end-of-life issues is becoming necessary.

At Saint Vincent, we created this website speciﬁcally to help you start the conversation with your aging loved ones. By preparing in advance, you can help reduce the doubt or anxiety related to making decisions for your family members when they cannot speak for themselves. The time to document their end-of-life care wishes is now—so you can ensure that they will receive the high-quality, compassionate care they deserve.

Simply click the resources tab to get started.
